At CyberQ Academy, we aim to build the next generation of tech talent through, training, mentorship, exposure, hackathons and opportunities in the various sectors of the tech world.

How We Nurture Talents

Besides organizing this yearly events, CyberQ Academy mentors also provide spaces and a comfortable environment where youths and other interested individuals can learn technical skills.

Moreover, they also run a competition called "Capture the Flag", where participants are challenged with programming puzzles they need to solve in a timed competition.

Winners are then awarded and recognized for their exemplary performance in solving the puzzles...
